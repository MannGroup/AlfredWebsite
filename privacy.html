Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Home Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy describes how Alfred SMS Butler Services ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our SMS messaging features and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Phone numbers - Phone numbers you provide when you opt in to receive SMS messages through our website at https://manngroup.github.io/AlfredWebsite/index.html
  • Message content - The content of SMS messages you send to us and messages we send to you, including appointment reminders, interactive chat responses, and service notifications
  • Message metadata - Technical information about messages, including timestamps, delivery status, message identifiers, and similar data
  • Opt-in records - Records of when and how you consented to receive messages, including timestamps and IP addresses
  • Interaction data - Information about your interactions with our service, such as message frequency and response patterns

How We Collect Information

We collect information directly from you when you:

  • Voluntarily opt in to receive SMS messages through our website by entering your phone number and checking the consent checkbox
  • Send SMS messages to our service
  • Reply to messages we send you (including STOP, HELP, or other responses)

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send and receive SMS messages you request, including appointment reminders and interactive chat responses
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our SMS reminder and messaging services
  • Prevent abuse, spam, or fraud and to troubleshoot technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
  • Maintain records of consent as required by telecommunications regulations

Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to deliver SMS messages and operate our services:

  • Twilio - We use Twilio, a third-party SMS service provider, to deliver SMS messages to you. Twilio processes phone numbers and message content as necessary to transmit messages and provide delivery reporting. Twilio's use of your information is governed by their privacy policy, available at https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy

These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only to provide services to us and are prohibited from using your information for their own purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With service providers (such as Twilio) strictly for delivering and operating messaging services
  • If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

Data Retention

We retain message data and opt-in records only as long as reasonably necessary for:

  • Service operation and delivery
  • Troubleshooting and technical support
  • Compliance with legal obligations and telecommunications regulations
  • Prevention of fraud and abuse

After this period, data is deleted or anonymized. If you opt out by replying STOP, we will stop sending messages but may retain records of your opt-in and opt-out as required by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure transmission of data using encryption
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

Opt-Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you opt out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us, except for a final confirmation message.

Help

For help or to request information about your account, reply HELP to any message, or contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our service after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
