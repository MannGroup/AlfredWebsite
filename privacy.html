This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our SMS messaging features and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Phone numbers used for SMS communication
  • Message content you send to us and messages we send to you
  • Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status, and similar technical data)

How We Use Information

  • To send and receive SMS messages you request
  • To operate, test, maintain, and improve messaging features
  • To prevent abuse, spam, or fraud and to troubleshoot issues

Service Providers

We use third-party providers to deliver SMS messages (for example, Twilio). These providers process phone numbers and message data as needed to transmit messages and provide delivery reporting.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers strictly for delivering and operating messaging services, or if required by law.

Opt-Out and Help

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Data Retention

We retain message data only as long as reasonably necessary for testing, troubleshooting, compliance, and service operation, then delete or anonymize it.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@yourdomain.com
